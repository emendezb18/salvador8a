Joya de Ceren

The ancient maya civilization in El Salvador

Joya de Ceren is a archaeological site in department of La Libertad, it has over 1,400 year and it is world Heritage Site since 1993 it is a important for people of El Salvador because it conserved almost the same form since it was made, this happened because it was cover with cinder of a volcano explosion of the volcano of “la calera”, it was made by Maya’s that live in Central America. It was discovered by a tractor that was digging in the ground and found the top of a house, so it was investigated by Payson Sheets of the university of Colorado that found the aproxímate age of the house. After this discovered the excavation started, it take some years to finish the excavation because the houses were 5 meters in the ground, when it was finished people can enter to the mesuem and the archieological part of the place.

If you want to go there you should catch a flight to San Salvador and drive 82.3 km or 1 hour and 45 minutes to Joya de Ceren. In this place you can learn about how the Maya civilization lived 1,400 years ago. The museum has a lot of ancient artefacts and the explanation of how they were used years ago. You should visit it know and see the ancient construction of maya’s civilization.
