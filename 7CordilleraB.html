Cordillera del Balsamo (Sonsonote and La libertad)

The balsamo forest

Cordillera del balsamo is a group of mountains that are named like this because it has mostly this type of tree, it is important to people of El Salvador because this place is one of the reasons that tree of balsamo is the second most important tree of El Salvador know like one of the two nacional trees, the balsamo tree is behind maquilishuat that is the most important for people that live in El Salvador. The “cordillera” is also important for the balance of nature in the southwest area of El Salvador because is the initial of some rivers like Acelhuate and Sucio that go until the Pacific Ocean, also has a lot of species of plants, birds and insects giving biodiversity and attracting a lot of tourists to see all the nature of the place giving a big area of free forest that refresh and give more beauty to El Salvador.
