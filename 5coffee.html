The coffee republic President Rafael Zaldívar, who replaced Santiago González in 1876 decreed laws that annulled the system of communal lands, that was been in the country since colonial times. This decree allowed a few families to have most of the land. Zaldívar was murder in 1885, he was replaced by General Francisco Menéndez, who change in the 1886 the Constitution, based on liberal principles. Because of this, European families go to the country and make themselves in a powerful economic position because of their knowledge of the market. These families make money in the area of ​​commerce and in the production of coffee. These families were the most important of the country, and had the control of the political parties of El Salvador. Known as the coffee republic, this period started in 1898 and finished in 1931.
