On the heights of El Salvador

In Chatalengo, El Salvador, you willfindlocated the highest mountain in El Salvador,. It is also a transnational mountain because it is shared with Honduras. This is a beautiful place where you can do different activities and see a beautiful landscape. It is also made up of a beautiful humid forest which contains ADJ trees such as pine, oak, and cypress. This mountain has an elevation of 2,730 meters. In the past this area had high volcanic activity, so this mountain is composed of this type of rock. The soil is therefore quite fertile and the altitude allows great humidity and rainfall. As a result, throughout history many events related to its climate have been reported, for example: In January 1956, in El Pital the lowest temperature recorded in El Salvador occurred: -6.1 degrees Celsius.
