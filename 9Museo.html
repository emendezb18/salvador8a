National Museum of Anthropology (San Salvador)

The most important museum of El Salvador

The National Museum of Anthropology is located on Avenida La Revolución San Salvador, El Salvador. This museum has a big collection of archaeological, and historical objects that gives a look to the past from pre-Hispanic periods to the present. The museum gives visual and historical moments of a long human occupation of the Salvadoran territory. It is managed by the Ministry of Culture of El Salvador.

If you want to go there you should first take a flight to San Salvador and drive to the place that is in the same city, you should go to learn about history of El Salvador and see the different things that ancient civilizations use.
